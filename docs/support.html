FAQs

  • Is Berani an emergency service? No. If you are in danger, call your local emergency number immediately.
  • Where are my reports stored? On your device unless you choose to share or back them up.
  • What does the AI see? Only the text (and optional photos) you submit for a summary, sent securely over HTTPS to generate the result.
  • How do I delete my data? Open the app and delete the report or photo from the Vault.
