Privacy Policy – Berani

Effective date: 23 August 2025

Berani helps users document incidents safely. This Privacy Policy explains what data is handled by the app and how it is used.

Data You Provide

  • Text you type into reports and notes
  • Photos you choose to attach

How Data Is Used

  • Your reports are stored on your device unless you explicitly share or back them up.
  • When you request an AI summary, the relevant text and optionally images are sent securely over HTTPS to our processing service solely to generate the summary and return results.

Retention

  • On-device data remains until you delete it from the app.
  • AI processing data is retained only as long as necessary to deliver the summary and maintain service reliability.

Security

  • All network communication uses HTTPS.
  • We recommend securing your device with a passcode/biometrics.

Your Choices

  • You may delete reports/photos from the app at any time.
  • You may choose not to use the AI summary feature.

Children

Berani is intended as a documentation tool and does not contain advertising or in-app purchases.

Contact

Email: help@berani.app

This policy may be updated as features evolve.
